Offensive words and phrases

Protect yourself from comments and message requests that contain offensive words, phrases or emojis.

Hide comments

Comments that may be offensive will automatically be hidden in a separate section of your posts, reels and videos.

Advanced comment filtering

Additional comments that may contain offensive words and phrases will be hidden. You can always review and unhide individual comments.

Hide message requests

Message requests that may be offensive will be moved to the hidden requests folder. We’ll also filter notifications for these messages.
